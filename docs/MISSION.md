# MTVL AI / Canoniga Mission

## Objective

Accelerate ALS research by reducing scientific uncertainty, prioritizing testable hypotheses, and supporting investigator decision-making with evidence-grounded local intelligence.

## Success criteria

- Evidence claims are traceable to sources with explicit confidence and contradiction tracking
- Hypothesis queue surfaces testable directions with supporting and contradictory evidence
- Human reviewers can approve, reject, or withhold promotion through explicit gates
- Automation runs produce replay-stable reports that pass configured quality gates

## Scope boundaries

Canoniga provides **research assistance** and **scientific discovery support**. It does **not**:

- Diagnose patients
- Recommend individual clinical treatment
- Replace experimental validation or peer review
- Autonomously declare cures or clinical readiness

## Responsibilities

- Ingest and normalize public biomedical evidence
- Maintain longitudinal confidence and contradiction models
- Generate investigator-readable synthesis with citations
- Flag high-risk causal claims and stale evidence sources

## Out of scope

- Direct patient care workflows
- Regulatory submission automation
- Cloud-only proprietary model dependencies
